SweetWorks Pastry Shop

Python Flask Bootstrap JavaScript Render

Problem

SweetWorks is an artisan pastry shop in Mati City, Davao Oriental. The owner needed a website that captures the warmth and craftsmanship of their brand while providing an online storefront for product discovery and custom cake inquiries.

Approach

Before writing code, I spent time understanding the brand and its customers. SweetWorks isn't just a bakery — it's a place for custom-designed cakes for weddings, birthdays, and special occasions. Every design decision came from that understanding.

  • Warm color palette — inspired by the shop's interior, using muted tones that feel inviting
  • Product-first layout — catalog and gallery are the focal points
  • Inquiry-driven CTA — custom cakes require consultation, so calls-to-action lead to contact rather than direct purchase

Implementation

Flask powers the backend with a product catalog structure. Bootstrap ensures responsive design — crucial since many customers browse on mobile. The site is deployed on Render's free tier.

Result

The site is live and the owner has received positive feedback from customers. It serves as both a digital storefront and a brand platform that communicates the personal touch SweetWorks is known for.
